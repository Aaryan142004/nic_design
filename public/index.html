    Why This Visualization?

    This project aims to present a clear, interactive view of state-wise political trends and women's representation in India. With intuitive visuals, district overlays, and real-time updates, it enables transparency and accessible data-driven insights for researchers, journalists, and citizens alike.

    Women Representation

    Visualizes the state-wise share of women elected to legislative assemblies in the 2022 elections.

    Party Dominance

    Color-coded maps show leading political parties in each state and their seat share.

    Real-time Dashboard

    Interactive pie charts and tooltips show a live summary of seats per party for any selected state.

    District Insight

    Hover and zoom into districts to discover granular administrative divisions of each state.
